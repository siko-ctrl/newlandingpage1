Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Salvium Protocol

What is Salvium Protocol?
Salvium Protocol is a decentralized finance (DeFi) platform that aims to provide private, secure, and efficient financial services. It combines cutting-edge privacy technology with DeFi functionality to create a comprehensive ecosystem for users.
How does Salvium ensure privacy?
Salvium uses advanced cryptographic techniques and zero-knowledge proofs to ensure transaction privacy. All transactions on the platform are encrypted and untraceable, while still maintaining the ability to verify their validity.
What are SAL tokens?
SAL tokens are the native utility tokens of the Salvium Protocol. They are used for governance, staking, and accessing various features within the ecosystem. Holders can participate in protocol decisions and earn rewards.
Where can I buy SAL tokens?
SAL tokens are available on several exchanges including Seven Seas, TradeOgre, XeggeX, nonkyc, and CoinEx. Visit our Exchanges page for direct links to trading pairs.
How can I participate in the Salvium ecosystem?
You can participate by holding SAL tokens, providing liquidity to pools, participating in governance, or using the various DeFi services offered by the protocol. Join our community channels to stay updated on the latest opportunities.
